Bio

Openness is the recording project of Keelan McDorman, brought to life through collaboration with a plethora of musicians. These dreamy, psychedelic tunes will transport you out of your thoughts while still providing catchy pop melodies to grab hold of on the way out of your head. The name is the essence of the music, for it is created from a place of openness...openness of consciousness, mind, heart, and soul. That openness translates to the listener. When music is created genuinely, it will be received genuinely. Everything about each tune is entirely up for interpretation; take from it what you will. Keelan feels the songs come through him rather than from him, almost as if he is a musical messenger.

Each Openness track is artfully crafted, recorded and produced in Keelan’s home, “The Open Nest” cottage in Denver, Colorado. He typically plays drums, keys, and sings the songs, though his instrumental horizons are ever expanding. Nevertheless, Openness will never cease to be an adventure of collaboration. In 8 months, Openness has released 23 songs, featuring 19 different musicians. That gives you an idea of the trajectory of Openness. This musical message is stamped for priority delivery.
